q2 - Indentation, comments, and naming conventions in Python.

Answer - 

Indentation:

Use 4 spaces per indentation level.
Avoid mixing tabs and spaces.
Line Length:

Limit lines to 79 characters.
Use parentheses to break long lines.

Comments:

Use clear and concise comments to explain the purpose of code.
Avoid unnecessary comments.
Use docstrings to document modules, classes, and functions.
